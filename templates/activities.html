How We Can Inc, works

We as a Non- governmental and global humanitarian association, focuses on people and communities, we work with every Institutions and Individuals for society improvement and the wellbeing of all, which is depends on everyone of us ensuring that we all feel that we have a stake in it and do not feel excluded from the mainstream of society. This is requires that all of us but, particularly the most vulnerable have equal opportunities to improve or maintain their wellbeing, We take action on issues confronting humanity.
