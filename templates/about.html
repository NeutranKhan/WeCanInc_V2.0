Working together with the local people

The local people play the main role in We Can Inc assistance program. Local staff lead program implementation with guidance from local professionals under the management of experienced international staff.

Focusing on forgotten peoples and regions

Not all areas in need receive media attention. We Can Inc, implements its assistance programs even in regions where there has been little or no media coverage, especially for vulnerable populations.

Building and maintaining self-reliance

We Can Inc, promotes self-reliance of those people in need. In that light, We Can Inc, programs take a long view and employ mechanisms that maximize the use of people's ability and the will of humans to live. The experience and expertise of We Can Inc, make this possible.

We achieve the best at minimum cost

For successful program implementation at minimum cost, we obtain all possible resources locally, both human and material. In this way the potential capabilities of an area can be developed effectively and economically.
