Volunteering with WE CAN INC.

We Can Inc works with volunteers primarily through the We Can Inc. Volunteers (WCIVP) programm. If you have an undergraduate degree, several years of work experience and are interested in volunteer opportunities around the world, you may be eligible to become a member. The WCIVP programm recruits and places volunteers in assignments throughout the system, including at We Can Inc. We Can Inc. does not recruit members directly but through the WCIVP programm. To be considered, please contact us.

Why Volunteer at WE CAN INC.

  • Because your're needed! No work is small, and volunteers are extremely valuable resources you can give to society.
  • If you actively work/volunteer will help you to make a positive contribution in the growth and development of the society.
  • You contribute in changing lives.
  • And better networking.

How Do I Volunteer?

Volunteering with We Can Inc. means you get the tools, resources and support you need to become an advocate, activist and humanitarians for social human work activities, fight against the injustice of global poverty and inspires sustainable democratic development by creating a communication to expose global environment problems and promote solutions that are essential to the world. From giving a few hours to making a life-long commitment, hundreds of people of all ages, genders and backgrounds across Africa and the world at large are making a huge contribution and creating a better future for others. Our Mission

By chances are you've heard of this events. maybe you'll walked it. why not try your hand as a volunteer? find a walk near you by using your expertise for a good cause in a diverse set of impactful means.Our mission is to create excellent society by combating impunity through the fight for equal access to justice, Economic empowerment of all citizens in Africa and the world by alleviate poverty and inspires sustainable democratic development and creating a communication to expose global environment problems and promote solutions that are essential to the world.

Our Vision

To help people throughout the world realize their full potential by bringing inspiration and innovation to every person in the world and built a future in which people live in harmony with nature and seek a world of tolerance, hope and social justice where poverty has been overcome and people live in dignity and security, with a just world that values and conserves nature

Our Goals

Our goals is to promote great opportunities for those in need with access to the right resources, so people can become empowered by their own abilities and gain the confidence to fulfill their potential. By further a social cause and provide a public benefit in improving the state of the natural environment, and encouraging the observance of human rights, in improving the welfare of the disadvantaged, or representing a corporate agenda.
